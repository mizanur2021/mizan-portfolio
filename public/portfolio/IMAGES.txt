HOW TO USE YOUR IMAGES
-----------------------
After dropping your images here, open:
  data/content.ts

Find the project you want to update and replace the Unsplash URL with the local path.
Example:

  images: [
    "/portfolio/surgeon-kibria-1.jpg",
    "/portfolio/surgeon-kibria-2.jpg",
    "/portfolio/surgeon-kibria-3.jpg",
  ],

The path always starts with /portfolio/ (no "public" prefix needed).
